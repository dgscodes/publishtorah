About the newsletter

ℹ︎

This weekly newsletter was started in 2022 for the grade 7 & 8 davening group.

It features a weekly Dvar Torah, jokes and helps people get involved in the tefillah service. The aim is to be an all inclusive, fun newsletter that utilises technology for the good.
